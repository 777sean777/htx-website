Roll-to-Roll Coating Systems (1.6m)

Wide-format vacuum sputtering systems engineered for stable long-run operation and precise tension control across delicate substrates.

System Overview

HTX Roll-to-Roll Coating Systems are designed for wide-format substrates up to 1.6 meters, focusing on transport stability and coating uniformity during extended operation.

The system architecture prioritizes mechanical balance, controlled tension, and repeatable deposition behavior under vacuum environments.

Design Focus

  • Wide-Format Transport Stability:
    Mechanical design optimized to maintain alignment and flatness across large-area substrates.
  • Precise Tension Control:
    Engineered tension management for thin, flexible, or surface-sensitive materials.
  • Long-Run Operational Reliability:
    Designed for extended coating cycles with consistent process behavior.
  • Vacuum Sputtering Integration:
    Fully compatible with sputtering-based deposition processes.

Typical Applications

  • Functional textile and fabric coating
  • Flexible substrate surface modification
  • Large-area material development and pilot-scale production
