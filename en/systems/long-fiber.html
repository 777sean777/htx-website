Long Fiber Coating Systems

Batch-type vacuum sputtering for long synthetic fibers used in chemical fiber applications.

System Overview

HTX Long Fiber Systems are engineered specifically for the vacuum sputtering of synthetic (chemical) fibers. Utilizing a batch-type processing architecture, we prioritize process stability over continuous line speed. This approach ensures high repeatability and ease of maintenance for specialized functional textile production.

Design Focus

  • Batch-Type Vacuum Sputtering Architecture: Optimized for controlled, high-quality deposition cycles.
  • Long Fiber Handling (Chemical Fiber): Specialized tension mechanisms designed for synthetic fiber properties.
  • Repeatable Operation: System logic ensures consistent coating thickness batch after batch.
  • Maintainability: Modular vacuum chamber design for simplified target replacement and cleaning.

Coating Metals

  • Silver (Ag)
  • Copper (Cu)
  • Stainless Steel (SS)
  • Titanium (Ti)

Typical Applications

  • Antibacterial & hygiene-related textile components
  • Thermal-related textile layers, inserts, and linings
  • Surface functionalization for industrial technical textiles

Collaboration Approach

We do not adopt a "test everything" approach. Instead, we engage in focused technical dialogue to evaluate feasibility before sampling. Our engineering team works with clients to determine if our batch-sputtering process aligns with the physical constraints of the specific fiber material.
