Automation Integration & Equipment Development

Engineering integration for custom equipment development—mechanical design, controls, and process integration.

System Overview

HTX provides automation integration and equipment development services for research-driven, application-oriented, and special-specification equipment projects. We combine mechanical engineering, electrical controls, and system integration capabilities to help customers translate requirements and concepts into operable equipment.

We are especially effective when requirements are not fully defined at the beginning—working with customers to shape system architecture, process configuration, and key modules, then advancing through testable, iterative engineering steps toward a deliverable system.

Design Focus

  • Mechanical & Controls Integration:
    In-house capability across mechanical structure, electrical control, and system bring-up for custom equipment.
  • Vacuum / Plasma Process Integration Experience:
    Practical integration know-how for vacuum hardware and plasma process platforms.
  • Roll-to-Roll Process & Handling Integration:
    Support for wide-format or continuous handling concepts through modular configuration and integration.
  • Prototype-to-Engineering Iteration Support:
    From validation to refinement—focused on making systems operable, testable, and deliverable.

Collaboration Scope & Applicable Projects

HTX welcomes collaboration with research institutes, academic organizations, governmental or corporate research units, and in-house R&D teams. Projects with clear objectives and an open technical framework are well suited for joint development.

HTX is often engaged when standard equipment platforms do not fully address the application need—particularly where architecture, process integration, and engineering implementation still offer meaningful design flexibility.

Typical Applications

  • Custom equipment development and special-specification machines
  • Robotic integration for equipment automation projects
  • Vacuum / plasma-related equipment platforms and module integration
  • Roll-to-Roll process integration and pilot systems
  • Prototype-to-engineering iteration projects
