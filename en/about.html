About HTX

HTX is the advanced surface engineering platform operated by Huntertex Corp.

We specialize in vacuum-based coating systems and functional material processing, supporting applications that require stable, repeatable, and production-ready solutions. Our capabilities include vacuum sputtering systems, roll-to-roll processing, long-fiber coating, and customized equipment and process integration.

HTX does not position itself as a standard equipment supplier. Instead, we work closely with clients to define system architecture, process feasibility, and long-term operational requirements based on real manufacturing conditions.

Through structured engineering collaboration, we help translate technical concepts into systems that can be reliably implemented, maintained, and scaled over time.
